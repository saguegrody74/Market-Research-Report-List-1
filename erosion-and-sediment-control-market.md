<p><ul><li>Erosion Control</li><li>Sediment Control</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The erosion and sediment control market encompasses two primary types: erosion control and sediment control. Erosion control focuses on preventing soil loss from wind or water, utilizing methods such as vegetation, mats, and barriers. Sediment control, on the other hand, aims to manage and filter out sediment from runoff, employing techniques like silt fences and sediment basins. Both categories are essential for protecting water quality, maintaining land integrity, and ensuring compliance with environmental regulations during construction and land development activities.</p></p>
