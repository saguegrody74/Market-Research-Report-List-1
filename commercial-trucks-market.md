Medium-duty carriers, with a GVWR between 14,000 and 26,000 pounds, serve both commercial and municipal applications. Heavy-duty carriers exceed 26,000 pounds and are designed for long-haul transportation of heavy loads. Each category addresses specific operational needs and market demands, shaping logistics and distribution strategies.</p></p>
