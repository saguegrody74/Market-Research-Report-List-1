<p><p>The gas separation film market comprises several key types: hollow fiber, spiral wound, and others. Hollow fiber membranes utilize thin, elongated fibers that efficiently separate gases through selective permeability, making them ideal for applications like CO2 removal. Spiral wound membranes consist of flat sheets wrapped in a spiral configuration, optimizing space and enhancing gas flow rates. The "others" category includes innovative designs such as flat sheet and tubular membranes, which offer versatility for various separation processes across different industries.</p></p>
