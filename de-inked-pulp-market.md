<p><p>The de-inked pulp market consists of several processing methods, primarily mechanical and chemical pulping. Mechanical pulping involves physically grinding recycled paper to separate fibers, retaining more ink and brightness, suitable for lower-quality products. Chemical pulping uses chemical solutions to dissolve and remove ink and impurities, producing higher-quality pulp ideal for premium applications. Additionally, "others" encompass alternative technologies like enzymatic de-inking and various hybrid methods, catering to specialized uses and enhancing recycling efficiency while minimizing environmental impact.</p></p>
