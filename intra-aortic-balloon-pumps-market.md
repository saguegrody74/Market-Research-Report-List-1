<p><p>Intra-aortic balloon pumps (IABPs) are medical devices designed to support patients with circulatory shock or heart failure. They function by inflating and deflating a balloon within the aorta, which helps improve myocardial oxygen supply and reduces cardiac workload. IABPs are commonly employed in critical care settings, particularly during high-risk cardiac procedures or in patients awaiting surgeries such as heart transplants.</p><p>The Intra-Aortic Balloon Pumps Market is witnessing significant growth driven by factors such as an increasing prevalence of cardiovascular diseases, advancements in medical technology, and a rising geriatric population, which is more susceptible to heart-related conditions. Additionally, the growing awareness about early diagnosis and treatment is further fueling market demand. </p><p>Technological innovations, including miniaturized and portable IABP systems, are emerging trends that enhance patient mobility and comfort.
