<p><p>The Structure Tester market is categorized into two main types: Benchtop Structure Testers and Floor Structure Testers. In contrast, Floor Structure Testers are larger, designed to evaluate the integrity of floors and larger structures, often used in construction and civil engineering to ensure safety and compliance with standards. Both play crucial roles in structural analysis.</p></p>
