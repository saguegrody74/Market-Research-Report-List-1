<p><p>The planar lightwave circuit (PLC) splitters market is primarily categorized into two types: 1×N and 2×N PLC splitters. 1×N PLC splitters are designed to split one input signal into multiple output signals (N outputs), making them ideal for connecting a single source to several destinations. In contrast, 2×N PLC splitters manage two input signals and distribute them across multiple outputs, increasing flexibility in network design. Both types are crucial for efficient signal distribution in fiber optic networks.</p></p>
