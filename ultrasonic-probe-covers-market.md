<p><p>The ultrasonic probe covers market includes two main types: latex probe covers and latex-free probe covers. Latex probe covers are made from natural rubber latex, providing effective barrier protection during medical procedures while maintaining tactile sensitivity. However, latex allergies have spurred demand for latex-free probe covers, which are typically made from materials like polyurethane or PVC. These alternatives cater to patients with latex sensitivities and ensure safety and hygiene in various healthcare settings, making them essential in medical diagnostics and imaging.</p></p>
