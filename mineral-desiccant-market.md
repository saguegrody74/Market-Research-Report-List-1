<p><ul><li>Silica Gel Desiccants</li><li>Clay Desiccants</li><li>Others</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The mineral desiccant market is primarily categorized into three types: silica gel desiccants, clay desiccants, and others. Silica gel desiccants are highly effective in moisture absorption, making them ideal for packaging and storage applications. Clay desiccants, made from natural clay, are cost-effective and offer good moisture control, often used in food and pharmaceuticals. The "others" category includes various desiccants like zeolites and molecular sieves, which are utilized in specialized applications requiring specific absorption properties and performance characteristics.</p></p>
