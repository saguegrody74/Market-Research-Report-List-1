<p><p>The Soft Acrylic Films market is categorized primarily into two types: Single-Side Coated and Double-Side Coated films. Single-Side Coated films feature an acrylic coating on one side, providing specific adhesion and protection capabilities while maintaining clarity and flexibility. In contrast, Double-Side Coated films have coatings on both sides, enhancing durability and offering greater versatility for applications like signage, displays, and protective overlays. These types cater to diverse industry needs, from automotive to electronics, based on performance requirements.</p></p>
