<p><p>Perovskite solar cells for the automotive market are classified into two main types: planar and mesoscopic. Planar perovskite solar cells feature a flat structure that enhances efficiency and ease of integration into vehicle designs, making them suitable for sleek surfaces. In contrast, mesoscopic perovskite solar cells utilize a porous scaffold to enhance light absorption and electron transport, which allows for greater energy conversion efficiency. Both types aim to provide lightweight, flexible, and efficient energy solutions for automotive applications.</p></p>
