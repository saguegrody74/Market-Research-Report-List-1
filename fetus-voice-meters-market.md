<p><strong>The Fetus voice Meters Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Doppler Fetus-voice Meters</li><li>Passive Fetus-voice Meters</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The fetus voice meters market comprises two main types: Doppler fetal monitors and passive fetal monitors. Doppler fetal monitors utilize ultrasound technology to detect and amplify the fetal heartbeat, providing real-time audio feedback for expectant parents and healthcare providers. These devices are popular due to their accuracy and ease of use. In contrast, passive fetal voice meters do not emit ultrasound waves; instead, they rely on external sound detection methods, offering a simpler and often less expensive alternative for monitoring fetal activity.</p></p>
