In contrast, liquid urea fertilizers are in a dissolved form, allowing for efficient nutrient absorption and application through irrigation systems. Both types cater to different agricultural needs and methods, with granular being popular for bulk application and liquid offering versatility for precision farming techniques.</p></p>
