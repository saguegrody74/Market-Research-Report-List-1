<p><ul><li>Linear Sortation Systems</li><li>Loop Sortation Systems</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The automated sortation system market is divided into two main types: linear sortation systems and loop sortation systems. Linear sortation systems operate on a straight path, efficiently directing items to various destinations, ideal for high-throughput environments. Conversely, loop sortation systems feature a circular design, allowing for more flexible item routing and minimizing space requirements. Both systems enhance operational efficiency, reduce labor costs, and improve accuracy in logistics and distribution, catering to diverse industries such as e-commerce, retail, and warehousing.</p></p>
