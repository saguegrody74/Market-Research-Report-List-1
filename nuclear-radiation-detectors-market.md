<p><strong>The Nuclear Radiation Detectors Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Gas Ionization Detectors</li><li>Semiconductor Detectors</li><li>Scintillation Detectors</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The nuclear radiation detectors market encompasses three primary types: Gas Ionization Detectors, Semiconductor Detectors, and Scintillation Detectors. Gas Ionization Detectors operate by ionizing gas molecules to detect radiation, making them suitable for a variety of applications. Semiconductor Detectors utilize semiconductor materials to directly convert radiation into an electrical signal, offering high sensitivity and resolution. Scintillation Detectors employ scintillating materials that emit light when exposed to radiation, which is then converted into an electrical signal, ideal for various detection scenarios.</p></p>
