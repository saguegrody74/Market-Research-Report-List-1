<p><p>The multimode optical fiber patch cord market comprises several types, each designed for specific applications. FC type fiber patch cords feature a screw-lock design for stability, commonly used in high-vibration environments. SC type patch cords offer a push-pull coupling mechanism, known for their low insertion loss. ST type cords utilize a bayonet-style connector, ideal for data communication. LC type patch cords are compact and favored for high-density installations. Other types include MTP/MPO connectors, providing versatile solutions for various networking needs.</p></p>
