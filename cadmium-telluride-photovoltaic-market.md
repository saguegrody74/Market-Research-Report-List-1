<p><p>The Cadmium Telluride (CdTe) photovoltaic market is characterized by three main types: single crystals, large grains, and standard cells. Single crystal CdTe offers high efficiency due to its uniform structure but is costly to produce. Large grain CdTe strikes a balance between efficiency and cost, allowing for more affordable manufacturing while maintaining decent performance. Standard cells, often used in mass production, offer lower efficiency but are economically viable and widely adopted, making them a popular choice in the solar market.</p></p>
