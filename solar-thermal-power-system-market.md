<p><strong>Market Segmentation</strong></p>
<p><strong>The Solar Thermal Power System Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Parabolic Trough Type</li><li>Power Tower Type</li><li>Dish Type</li><li>Fresnel Type</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The solar thermal power system market comprises several key types: </p><p>1. **Parabolic Trough Type** utilizes curved mirrors to focus sunlight onto a receiver pipe, generating steam for electricity.</p><p>2. **Power Tower Type** employs a central tower surrounded by mirrors, directing sunlight to a receiver at the top, producing high-temperature steam.</p><p>3. **Dish Type** features a parabolic dish that concentrates solar energy onto a receiver, driving a Stirling engine for power generation.</p><p>4. **Fresnel Type** uses flat mirrors to focus sunlight onto a receiver, providing steam for turbines, known for its modular design and cost-effectiveness.</p></p>
