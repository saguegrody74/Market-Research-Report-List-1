<p><strong>The Pulsed Electric Field Ablation System Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Reversible Electroporation Technology</li><li>Irreversible Electroporation</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Pulsed Electric Field Ablation System market includes two main types: reversible electroporation and irreversible electroporation. Reversible electroporation temporarily permeabilizes cell membranes, allowing for the introduction of drugs or genetic material, making it ideal for therapeutic applications. In contrast, irreversible electroporation causes permanent cell damage, leading to cell death, which is primarily utilized in cancer ablation procedures. Both technologies leverage electric fields to achieve distinct outcomes, catering to various medical needs and treatment strategies.</p></p>
