<p><p>The low voltage DC circuit breaker market includes two primary types: Miniature Circuit Breakers (MCBs) and Molded Case Circuit Breakers (MCCBs). MCBs are designed for overload and short-circuit protection in low current applications, offering compact size and quick response. Conversely, MCCBs cater to higher current ratings, providing adjustable settings for diverse industrial applications and enhanced protection. Both types are essential for safeguarding electrical circuits in various sectors, including residential, commercial, and industrial environments, ensuring system safety and reliability.</p></p>
