<p><p>The almond powder market features two primary types: blanched almond powder and natural almond powder. Blanched almond powder is made from almonds that have had their skins removed, resulting in a finer texture and lighter color, making it ideal for baking and desserts. Natural almond powder, on the other hand, retains the almond skins, offering a coarser texture and a richer flavor, suitable for health-conscious consumers and those seeking a more robust taste in recipes. Both types cater to diverse culinary applications.</p></p>
