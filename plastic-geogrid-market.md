<p><ul><li>Uniaxial Stretch Plastic Geogrid</li><li>Two-Way Stretch Plastic Geogrid</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The plastic geogrid market is categorized into two main types: uniaxial stretch plastic geogrids and two-way stretch plastic geogrids. Uniaxial stretch geogrids are designed to provide reinforcement primarily in one direction, suitable for applications like soil stabilization and base reinforcement. In contrast, two-way stretch geogrids offer reinforcement in both horizontal and vertical directions, enhancing load distribution and improving overall stability in diverse applications, including retaining walls and subgrade support, making them adaptable for varied engineering projects.</p></p>
