<p><p>The ultrasound transducers market is categorized into three main types: Linear Array, Convex Array, and Phased Array transducers. Linear Array transducers offer high-resolution imaging, ideal for superficial structures and vascular studies. Convex Array transducers provide a broader field of view, making them suitable for abdominal and obstetric imaging. Phased Array transducers utilize electronic steering to focus on specific areas, commonly used in cardiology and for echocardiograms. Each type serves distinct clinical needs, contributing to diverse diagnostic applications.</p></p>
