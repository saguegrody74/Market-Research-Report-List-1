<p><p>The PVC modifier market encompasses various types, each enhancing the properties of PVC. ACR (Acrylic Based Polymer) improves impact resistance and clarity, making it suitable for applications like windows. MBS (Methacrylate Butadiene Styrene) offers exceptional toughness and weather resistance, ideal for outdoor and automotive products. CPE (Chlorinated Polyethylene) enhances flexibility and UV resistance, commonly used in roofing and wiring applications. Other modifiers may include thermoplastic elastomers and elastomeric compounds, offering specialized characteristics for diverse industrial uses.</p></p>
