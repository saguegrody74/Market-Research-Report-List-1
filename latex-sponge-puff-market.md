<p><p>The latex sponge puff market encompasses various materials, including NR (Natural Rubber), NBR (Nitrile Butadiene Rubber), and SBR (Styrene Butadiene Rubber). NBR, a synthetic option, provides excellent oil resistance and durability, suitable for industrial uses. SBR, another synthetic variant, offers good abrasion resistance and is cost-effective, often used in consumer products. Each type caters to specific market needs based on performance and application.</p></p>
