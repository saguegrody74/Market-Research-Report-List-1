<p><p>Winged IV needles come in two main market types: double wing and single wing. Double wing needles feature two flexible "wings" on either side, providing better stabilization and control during insertion, making them ideal for continuous infusions or blood draws. In contrast, single wing needles have one wing, offering a more streamlined design that allows for easy handling in tight spaces, often preferred in short-term applications or for patients with smaller veins. Both types prioritize comfort and efficiency in intravenous therapy.</p></p>
