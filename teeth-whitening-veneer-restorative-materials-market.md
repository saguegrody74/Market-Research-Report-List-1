<p><p>The teeth whitening veneer restorative materials market features two primary types: resin veneers and porcelain veneers. Resin veneers are made from composite materials, offering affordability and ease of application but may not be as durable or stain-resistant as porcelain. Porcelain veneers, on the other hand, are crafted from high-strength ceramic, providing a natural appearance and superior durability, making them more resistant to wear and staining. Both types cater to different patient needs, preferences, and budget considerations in cosmetic dentistry.</p></p>
