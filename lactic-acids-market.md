<p><p>The lactic acid market comprises three main types: L-lactic acid, D-lactic acid, and DL-lactic acid. L-lactic acid is predominantly used in food, pharmaceuticals, and biodegradable plastics due to its non-toxic nature. D-lactic acid, while less common, finds applications in specialized industrial processes. DL-lactic acid, a racemic mixture of both isomers, serves as a versatile compound in various chemical applications. Each type plays a distinct role in sectors such as food, healthcare, and sustainable materials.</p></p>
