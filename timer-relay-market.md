<p><p>The Timer Relay Market is segmented into two primary types: DIN Rail Mount and Panel Mount. DIN Rail Mount timers are designed for installation on standard rail systems within electrical cabinets, offering easy integration and space efficiency. In contrast, Panel Mount timers are directly attached to control panels, providing user-friendly access and visibility for operators. Both types serve critical roles in automation, controlling timing functions in various applications, ensuring precise operations in industrial and commercial environments.</p></p>
